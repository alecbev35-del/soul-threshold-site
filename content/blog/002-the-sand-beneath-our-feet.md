Life was never working that way.

The wave returns.
And when it does, the ground beneath you moves -
with certainity.

What you believed was stable reveals itself as illusion.



The Grounded Truth

Life is not fixed.
It does not disappear - it transforms.

What leaves returns in another form,
another moment you did not expect.

You are not losing life as it shifts beneath you.
You are witnessing it move.
