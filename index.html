I work with people navigating grief, fear, and patterns that keep repeating — the kind that don't resolve with more information, another book, or positive thinking.

What I offer is direct, intuitive mentoring. Not therapy. Not coaching in the conventional sense. A clear-eyed conversation about what's actually happening, and what to do next.

I'm direct. I don't do spiritual bypassing and I won't validate a narrative you already know isn't true.

If you want comfort only, I'm not your person.

If you want clarity — and a next step — you're in the right place.
